All About
Me

Hey there! I'm Kaviyan Jeyakumar. Born and raised in Canada with a proud Sri Lankan heritage. When I'm not deep in my engineering coursework, you can usually find me with a good book, on the soccer field with friends, or at the gym. Lately, cooking has become a welcome hobby. I find it to be a uniquely relaxing activity that also provides a great sense of accomplishment.

Professionally, I'm an aspiring Electrical Engineer with a fascination for taking on big challenges. That's what led me to join the Orbital CubeSat team, where I'm currently working with an amazing group of people to build and test a satellite from the ground up. This experience has solidified my passion for the field, and I'm excited about a future where I can help design the complex systems that power modern technology, whether it's here on Earth or in orbit.
